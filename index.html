Why we started this THING

I have created a website as a project to give myself a purpose during COVID-19 pandemic. Watching 2 of my favorite podcast's ( 2 bears 1 cave & Bill Bert podcast) I saw how my favorite comedians have fun amongst each other by putting a comedic twists on current events as well as their own experiences, so I decided i can try see what is going on with my friends and family. Having spend a number of years working on cruise ships and relocating to USA I've came across to a number of set backs and ridicolous situations as well as my co-hosts Tomislav & Denis. So this website/podcast is gonna be a UNCENSORED with a wide range of topics from our own experiances
